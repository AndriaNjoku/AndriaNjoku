  ## ✊🏿 KarmaPolitics                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                 
  **KarmaPolitics is an evidence-led accountability platform for UK politics.**                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                 
  Politics is usually experienced as noise: a speech clip, a headline, a promise, a scandal—then nothing connecting it back to the people with   
power.                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                 
  KarmaPolitics is my attempt to make that connection visible.                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                 
  It brings together parliamentary activity, votes, questions, statements and evidence so people can follow:                                     
                                                                                                                                                 
  - what their MP is doing                                                                                                                       
  - what they have supported, opposed or avoided                                                                                                 
  - how that activity relates to the issues people actually care about                                                                           
  - the evidence behind a claim—not just the loudest version of it                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                 
  The goal is not to tell people what to think.                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                 
  The goal is to make political power more **legible**, more **personal**, and more **accountable**.                                             
                                                                                                                                                 
  > Less “trust me.” More “show me the record.”
